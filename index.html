About ITSA

ITSA is a student-driven body that enhances technical skills, leadership, and collaboration among IT students while bridging academics with real-world learning through events and workshops.

Mission

    Our mission is to create opportunities for students to learn beyond the classroom, build technical and leadership skills, and foster teamwork.

What We Do ?

The committee hosts competitions, showcases, talks, and cultural events to support students' overall growth.
